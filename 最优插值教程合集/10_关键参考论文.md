# 关键参考论文列表

## 海洋 EnOI 数据同化

### 海洋温盐剖面同化
- Fu, W., She, J., & Dobrynin, M. (2011). Application of an Ensemble Optimal Interpolation in a North/Baltic Sea Model: Assimilating Temperature and Salinity Profiles. *Ocean Modelling*, 40(3-4), 227-245.
  - 直接在北大西洋/波罗的海模型中使用 EnOI 同化 T/S 剖面
  - 分析了多变量特性和局部化方案

### 东南亚/南海 NEMO-EnOI
- (2025). Development and Application of Ensemble Optimal Interpolation Data Assimilation on the Southeast Asia Region and Southwestern South China Sea. *Applied Ocean Research*, 162.
  - 在 NEMO 海洋模型中集成 EnOI 代码
  - 温盐 RMSE 持续降低，SST 偏差 < ±0.5°C
  - 与你的场景高度相关（南海 + 温盐剖面）

### EnOI 偏差校正
- (2023). A Simple Bias Correction Scheme in Ocean Data Assimilation. *Journal of Marine Science and Engineering*, 11(1), 205.
  - 使用 EN4.2.2 数据集的 EnOI 同化
  - 分析偏差校正对热/盐含量趋势的影响

## IAP 数据集核心论文

1. **Cheng, L. & Zhu, J.** (2016). Benefits of CMIP5 Multimodel Ensemble in Reconstructing Historical Ocean Subsurface Temperature Variation. *Journal of Climate*, 29(15), 5393–5416.
2. **Cheng, L., Trenberth, K., Fasullo, J., et al.** (2017). Improved Estimates of Ocean Heat Content from 1960 to 2015. *Science Advances*, 3, e1601545.
3. **Cheng, L., Pan, Y., Tan, Z., et al.** (2024). IAPv4 Ocean Temperature and Ocean Heat Content Gridded Dataset. *Earth System Science Data*, 16, 3517–3546.

## 最优插值理论

- Daley, R. (1991). *Atmospheric Data Analysis*. Cambridge University Press. — OI 的经典教材
- Gandin, L. S. (1965). *Objective Analysis of Meteorological Fields*. — OI 的奠基性著作
- Kalnay, E. (2003). *Atmospheric Modeling, Data Assimilation and Predictability*. Cambridge University Press. — 数据同化标准教材

## 变分海洋插值（DIVA/DIVAnd）

- Barth, A., Beckers, J.-M., Troupin, C., et al. (2014). DIVAnd-1.0: n-Dimensional Variational Data Analysis for Ocean Observations. *Geoscientific Model Development*, 7, 225–241.
- Troupin, C., Barth, A., Sirjacobs, D., et al. (2012). Generation of Analysis and Consistent Error Fields Using the Data Interpolating Variational Analysis (DIVA). *Ocean Modelling*, 52-53, 90-101.

## 海洋数据格点化综述

- Wunsch, C. (1996). *The Ocean Circulation Inverse Problem*. Cambridge University Press. — 海洋反演经典
- Bennett, A. F. (2002). *Inverse Modeling of the Ocean and Atmosphere*. Cambridge University Press. — 海洋大气反演模型

## 机器学习/深度学习海洋插值

- Barth, A., Alvera-Azcárate, A., et al. (2020). DINCAE 1.0: A Convolutional Neural Network with Error Estimates to Reconstruct Sea Surface Temperature Satellite Observations. *Geoscientific Model Development*, 13, 1609–1622.
- Barth, A., Alvera-Azcárate, A., et al. (2022). DINCAE 2.0: Multivariate Convolutional Neural Network with Error Estimates. *Geoscientific Model Development*, 15, 2183–2202.
- Alvera-Azcárate, A., Barth, A., Rixen, M., & Beckers, J. M. (2005). Reconstruction of Incomplete Oceanographic Data Sets Based on EOF Decomposition. *Journal of Geophysical Research*, 110, C12011.
